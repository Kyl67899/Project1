Iceland

Iceland is a country of sharp contrast. A place where fire and ice co-exist. When dark winters are offset by the summer's midnight sun. A country where insular existence has spurred a rish and vibrant culture.

Things to do in Iceland

The Aurora Borealis takes its name from the Roman goddess of dawn, Aurora, and the Greek name for the north wind, Boreas. From september to april, Iceleand is a hotspot for this magnificent lightshow. The Aurora Borealis, more commonly refferred to as the Northern Lights is a natural phenomenon created when particles emitted by the sun interact with the atmosphere in the Earth's magnetic field. This releases energy, causing peculiar luminous green streaks across the skies
